Digital Electronics :


0s and 1s : The world of digital technology.


In this era of digitalization and automation, devices are being made smart. Smart devices build a smart system. Analog components and logic gates are the building blocks of digital components. The design must assure that the analog nature of the components doesn't dominate the desired digital behavior. Bad designs have intermittent problems commonly known as "glitches" which are unexpected combinations of logic states. Hardware engineers at Cdix have the right expertise on digital circuit design and choosing the right electronics components to produce high quality products. We help take our customer's business to a whole new revolution.

Analog & Mixed Signals :


The world is going wireless.


Due to coming IoT (Internet of Things), any sensors or digital electronic device that interacts with the real world will always need an analog and digital interface. To meet the increasing complexity and challenges in mixed-signal design, neither specialized analog nor digital skills alone can cover this since a broader knowledge of both domains are required. Our Hardware design teams are committed to sharing responsibility to meet multiple objectives for the entire project instead of owning just the analog or digital piece of it. For example, decisions allocating minimal area and power on choosing the right IC and electronic components need to be made jointly from a mixed-signal design perspective, to meet performance specifications that ensure full product signoff for both analog and digital functionality. This approach results in higher quality designs achieved in shorter time and with fewer iterations among design teams.
